About

Sun Clock is a 24-hour clock that displays the position of the sun, and sunrise, solar noon, sunset, golden hour, and twilight times for your current location.

If you are in the Northern Hemisphere, the Sun moves across the sky in a clockwise direction. Conversely, if you are in the Southern Hemisphere, the Sun moves anti-clockwise. This clock matches this by setting its direction of rotation based on your latitude[1], but you can change it in the settings if you don't like it.

1. Ideally you want the clock to turn in the same direction as the sun, regardless of which hemisphere you are in. If you are facing North when at your computer set it to anti-clockwise.

Privacy

We collect aggregate user stats but no other information is collected or stored. Your location data is private and is not sent to the server.
