About

Sun Clock is a 24-hour clock that displays the position of the sun, and sunrise, solar noon, sunset, golden hour, and twilight times for your current location.

If you are in the Southern Hemisphere, the Sun is generally[1] in the northern half of the sky, and travels across the sky anti-clockwise. Sun Clock replicates this, but you can change it in the settings if you don't like it.

1. i.e. more often than not. If you are south of the Tropic of Capricorn, then the Sun is always in the northern half of the sky.

Privacy

We collect aggregate user stats but no other information is collected or stored. Your location data is private and is not sent to the server.
